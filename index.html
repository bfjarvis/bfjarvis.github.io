Research on mobility, segregation, and methods for their analysis.

I am an associate professor at The Institute for Analytical Sociology at Linkoping University. My research examines ethnic segregation and patterns of residential and social mobility. I combine discrete choice models and agent-based simulations to make the link between micro behaviors and macro outcomes.

This site is a record of formal outputs and a home for short research notes. You can also find my CV and links to my Google Scholar and ORCID pages. And you can see what I am up to on GitHub or reach out to me by e-mail.
