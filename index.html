Research on mobility and segregation and the methods for analyzing them.

I am an associate professor at The Institute for Analytical Sociology at Linkoping University. My research examines ethnic segregation, residential mobility, and patterns of social mobility.

Methodologically, I use discrete choice models, agent-based models, and counterfactual simulations. This site is a home for formal outputs and reproducible vignettes that make the research process more transparent.
