Research on mobility and segregation and methods for their analysis.

I am an associate professor at The Institute for Analytical Sociology at Linkoping University. My research examines ethnic segregation, residential mobility, and patterns of social mobility.

Methodologically, I use discrete choice models, agent-based models, and counterfactual simulations. This site is record of formal outputs and a home for short research notes and data vignettes that make the research process more transparent.
